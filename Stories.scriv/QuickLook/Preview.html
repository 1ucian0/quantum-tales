      The Cowherd and the Weaver Girl


      The tale of the cowherd and the weaver girl is a love story between Zhīnǚ (织女; the weaver girl, symbolizing the star Vega) and Niúláng (牛郎; the cowherd, symbolizing the star Altair). Their love was not allowed; thus, they were banished to opposite sides of the heavenly river (symbolizing the Milky Way). Once a year, on the 7th day of the 7th lunar month, a flock of magpies would form a bridge to reunite the lovers for one day.
